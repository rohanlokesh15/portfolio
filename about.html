About Me

I build intelligent and visually engaging solutions that combine thoughtful design with robust engineering. My work spans:

  • Front-End Design — modern, accessible and responsive UI/UX design that works on all devices.
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning — predictive models, model evaluation, and deployment.
  • Data Analytics — data cleaning, exploration, visualization and deriving actionable insights.

  • Service Development — building APIs, microservices, Dockerized deployments and production-ready backends.

My approach: user-centered design + reproducible engineering. I like to take prototypes to production — from UI prototyping to scalable model serving.
